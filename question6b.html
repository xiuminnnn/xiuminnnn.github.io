Ingredients

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 stalks curry leaves

Cooking Steps

  1. saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant
  2. add in chicken
  3. saute until chicken releases oil
  4. add in water,tamarind paste,curry leaves,potato,onion,sugar and salt
  5. let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked throughly
  6. add in coconut milk
  7. cook until oil starts to separate
  8. remove and serve
